Create a topic:
kafka-topics.bat --create --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --partitions 1 --replication-factor 1 --topic topic_name

Describe topic:
kafka-topics.bat --describe --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --topic topic_name    

Consumer:
kafka-console-consumer.bat --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --topic topic_name --from-beginning

Producer:
kafka-console-producer.bat --broker-list localhost:9092 --topic topic_name

Start Zookeeper:
zookeeper-server-start.bat ..\..\config\zookeeper.properties

Start Kafka:
kafka-server-start.bat ..\..\config\server.properties

Listing topics:
kafka-topics.sh --list --zookeeper localhost:2181
